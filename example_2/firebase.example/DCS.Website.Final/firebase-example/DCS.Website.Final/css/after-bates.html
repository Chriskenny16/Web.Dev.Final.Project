Bates Alumni Career Paths

Our Digital and Computational Studies graduates pursue diverse career paths across technology, business, education, and creative industries. Our alumni have gone on to work at leading companies, startups, educational institutions, and non-profit organizations.

Software Development

Software Engineer

Design and build applications, systems, and services that solve real-world problems.

Alumni work at: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Local Startups

Full-stack Developer

Create both front-end user interfaces and back-end server logic for web applications.

Alumni work at: Salesforce, IBM, HubSpot, Educational Institutions

Mobile App Developer

Build applications for iOS, Android, and cross-platform mobile experiences.

Alumni work at: Apple, Financial Technology Companies, Healthcare Startups

Data Analysis

Data Scientist

Apply statistical analysis, machine learning, and programming to extract insights from data.

Alumni work at: Netflix, Healthcare Organizations, Research Institutions

Data Analyst

Interpret data to inform business decisions through visualization and reporting.

Alumni work at: Consulting Firms, Financial Institutions, Retail Companies

Business Intelligence Analyst

Transform complex data into actionable insights for strategic business planning.

Alumni work at: LinkedIn, Fortune 500 Companies, Government Agencies

Digital Content Creation

UX/UI Designer

Create intuitive and engaging digital experiences through user-centered design.

Alumni work at: Design Agencies, Tech Companies, E-commerce Businesses

Digital Media Specialist

Produce content for websites, social media, and multimedia platforms.

Alumni work at: Media Companies, Marketing Agencies, Educational Institutions

Content Strategist

Plan, develop, and manage content across digital channels to meet business objectives.

Alumni work at: Publishing Companies, Technology Firms, Non-profit Organizations
