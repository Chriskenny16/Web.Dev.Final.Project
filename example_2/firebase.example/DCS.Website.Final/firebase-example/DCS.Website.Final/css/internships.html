Industry Partnerships

The Digital and Computational Studies program maintains strong relationships with industry partners who regularly hire Bates students for internships and full-time positions.

Tech Company Partners

Our students have interned and worked at leading technology companies, including Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and numerous startups founded by Bates alumni.

Financial Services

We've established partnerships with financial institutions that value our students' combination of technical skills and liberal arts education for roles in fintech, data analysis, and software development.

Research Institutions

Students interested in research careers have opportunities at leading laboratories, universities, and research-focused organizations in fields like AI, data science, and computational biology.

Non-Profit Sector

For students passionate about social impact, we partner with non-profit organizations that need technical expertise in data analysis, digital communications, and technology implementation.
